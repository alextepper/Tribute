Oscar Isaac

Actor of the decade

Biography

Oscar Isaac, born Óscar Isaac Hernández Estrada on March 9, 1979, in Guatemala, is a Guatemalan-American actor known for his captivating performances on stage and screen.
From his breakout role in "Inside Llewyn Davis" to his charismatic turn in the "Star Wars" sequel trilogy, Isaac's versatility and dedication have earned him critical acclaim. With a magnetic presence and ability to bring complex characters to life, he continues to be one of the most talented and sought-after actors of his generation.
